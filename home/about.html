About Page

<

At i2Global we always believe that education is a process and learning is a journey of life time. We develop the young minds to embrace the learning opportunity and prepare them as lifelong LEARNERS.

This New Normal has bought in drastic changes and challenges in current education trends, we as an Educator s are coping up with changing reality and updating ourselves to prepare our children to face the exceptional challenges of the competitive future.


The School was founded with the aim of nurturing global ideals through qualitative education, blending Western standards with rich Indian values. I2global School aims at providing a safe, natural learning environment to nurture the children intellectually, emotionally, socially and physically through developing their personality to shape their creativity, cooperative skills, self-esteem, moral grounding and self-confidence.

We aim to identify and develop children’s inborn talents along with encouraging them to be respectful, considerate and responsible individuals. Our innovative teaching methods help develop children’s cognitive skills through Inquiry-based learning with active involvement of parents.


We cultivate a vibrant learning community that prepare the child to thrive in a dynamic world. We are revolutionising the early childhood education scenario in the country by introducing a novel method of pedagogy in pre-school.

We cater to the growing segment of quality conscious parents, who desire a holistic experience in the formative years of their child’s life. We are committed to provide world-class early childhood education with globally accepted, best teaching practices such as INQUIRY-BASED LEARNING AND THE REGGIO EMILIA APPROACH by following INTERNATIONAL CURRICULUM.

Regular ELF

12 months

Discover Node.js, the incredibly popular JavaScript runtime that enables you to build everything from command-line tools to web servers and APIs.
